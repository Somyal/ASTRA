# Astra Non-Goals

## Purpose

This document defines what Astra will not become. Every feature proposal must be checked here before it enters research, roadmap, or implementation. A feature that conflicts with a non-goal requires an explicit product decision to amend this document; it cannot enter by accumulation.

## Permanent non-goals

- **Not a second brain.** Astra does not attempt to store every thought, link every note, or replace a knowledge-management system.
- **Not a general productivity application.** It does not become an all-purpose task manager, project manager, habit tracker, or life dashboard.
- **Not a note-taking replacement.** Students may attach concise evidence or reflection, but Astra does not compete with dedicated notes tools.
- **Not a calendar replacement.** It may eventually exchange selected study commitments with calendars, but it does not own a user's whole schedule.
- **Not a social platform.** No feed, follower system, public leaderboard, comparison loop, or unmoderated accountability mechanic belongs in Astra's core.
- **Not a gamified habit machine.** Streaks, badges, rewards, and loss aversion must never define motivation or product success.
- **Not an AI chatbot for random conversation.** AI has bounded learning-record jobs: explain, summarize, or propose with evidence.
- **Not an AI authority, therapist, or substitute teacher.** Astra cannot diagnose, judge, manipulate, or claim personal understanding beyond evidence.
- **Not a cloud account requirement.** The core learning loop remains local-first and useful offline.
- **Not a feature showroom.** Internal tools, inactive experiments, and “coming soon” surfaces do not occupy student-facing navigation.
- **Not an opaque auto-scheduler.** Astra may recommend; it must explain and let the student decide.

## Boundary test for proposals

Before accepting a proposal, answer:

1. Does it strengthen Choose → Focus → Outcome → Learning Record → Next Action?
2. Does it turn Astra into one of the non-goal categories above?
3. Could a dedicated tool serve the need better without weakening Astra's core?
4. Does it preserve student attention, agency, ownership, and offline usefulness?

If the proposal fails any test, postpone or reject it. The burden of proof is on expansion, not restraint.
