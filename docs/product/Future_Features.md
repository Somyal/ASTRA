# Future Features Ledger

This ledger holds ideas that are not active work. A feature may move into delivery only when it passes the Product Recalibration v1 admission rule and its listed dependencies are complete. “Interesting” is not sufficient.

## Must have

These are core-loop capabilities and belong in the active product roadmap, not speculative feature work.

- **Stopwatch mode** — an upward-counting focus option for students who find countdowns stressful. Depends on the corrected session and Learning Record model.
- **Configurable recovery breaks** — duration and opt-out choices, provided they preserve a calm focus flow.
- **Automatic encrypted local backup rotation** — safe, verified snapshots with retention controls. Depends on complete transactional backup and restore.
- **Structured reflection/history timeline** — a searchable, student-owned record of prior outcomes and notes. Depends on the Learning Record and pagination.

## Should have

- **Transparent adaptive session-length suggestions** — proposes, never applies, a duration based on student-confirmed history. Depends on sufficient evidence and recommendation explanation.
- **Curriculum content packs and import** — supports more than the initial JEE data set. Depends on stable syllabus and content versioning.
- **Weekly learning review** — a concise review of commitments, outcomes, revisions, and changed plan. Depends on the Evidence Engine.
- **Accessible theme and motion options** — high contrast, reduced motion, and keyboard-first refinements. This is an ongoing acceptance criterion, not optional polish.

## Could have

- **Local ambient sound mixer** — multi-channel local audio. Only after it cannot distract from the core focus experience.
- **Calendar export/integration** — export planned learning actions or integrate selected commitments. Depends on transparent planning and explicit user control.
- **Mobile companion** — view plan, start a focused action, and record an outcome. Depends on mature local data ownership and a deliberate sync strategy.

## Experimental

- **Evidence-linked adaptive memory** — conservative proposed observations with sources, confidence, expiry, and correction controls. Depends on the Learning Record and privacy review.
- **Opt-in AI planning explanation** — natural-language explanation or proposal layered over deterministic recommendations. Depends on consent, offline-core behavior, and source attribution.
- **Privacy-preserving aggregate learning research** — only with explicit consent and legal/privacy review; never required for the core experience.

## Far future

- **Cross-device synchronization** — only after stable record identities, conflict semantics, complete export/restore, and a security model are established. CRDTs are not an active requirement.
- **Advanced AI companion** — only after evidence engine, adaptive memory, and safe proposal workflows prove value.
- **Dynamic lighting wallpapers** — postpone indefinitely; visual novelty is not a core learning benefit.
- **Plugin or marketplace ecosystem** — postpone indefinitely; extensibility is not a near-term student need.
- **Social/accountability features** — do not pursue without a separate safety, moderation, and anti-comparison product strategy.
