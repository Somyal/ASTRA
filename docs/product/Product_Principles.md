# Astra Product Principles

These principles govern every future product milestone.

1. **Astra is a Learning Operating System.** It helps turn a curriculum and evidence into the next honest learning action.
2. **The Learning Record is the source of truth.** Sessions, tasks, topic state, practice, and assessment are distinct facts.
3. **AI proposes; the student decides.** AI explains and suggests with evidence. It does not silently mutate academic truth.
4. **Local-first by default.** The core loop works offline, data stays owned by the student, and export/recovery are real promises.
5. **Every feature strengthens the learning loop.** Choose → Focus → Outcome → Learning Record → Next Action is the admission test.
6. **Calm over flashy.** Reduce cognitive load; do not use noise, urgency, or reward loops to create engagement.
7. **Trust over automation.** Correctness, recovery, clarity, and explanation are more valuable than an impressive autonomous behavior.
8. **Progress over productivity theater.** Time is useful evidence, never proof of learning or mastery.
9. **One polished experience beats ten unfinished ones.** Hide incomplete systems rather than turning them into expectation debt.
10. **The student retains agency.** Every recommendation is understandable, editable, deferrable, and correctable.
11. **Accessibility is product coverage.** If a student needs keyboard access, reduced motion, contrast, localization, or assistive support to participate, it is not optional polish.
12. **Remove complexity whenever possible.** Prefer a smaller, explicit model over a speculative abstraction, invisible automation, or duplicate source of truth.
13. **Be honest about what Astra knows.** Facts, student reports, deterministic rules, and AI proposals must never be presented as the same thing.
14. **Astra should disappear during real work.** The product succeeds when attention returns to learning, not to Astra.
